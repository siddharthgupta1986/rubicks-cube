# Make Wraith detection readable and avoidable

Void Wraiths will follow authored patrols and detect Aya through line of sight or close proximity. Detection must provide clear visual and audio warning before pursuit; after losing sight, a Wraith investigates Aya's last known position, searches temporarily, and eventually resumes patrol. Walls and closed doors block detection, and Wraiths never track Aya omnisciently. This favors understandable stealth-lite tension over unpredictable enemy behavior.
