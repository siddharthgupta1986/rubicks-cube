# Make exploration player-controlled and first-person

Cube Warden's campaign will use a real-time, freely walkable first-person Archive instead of an illustrated route with automatic node-to-node travel. Cube seals remain the means of progression: interacting with one opens the existing Rubik's Cube puzzle experience, and solving it changes or unlocks the world. This direction accepts substantially greater movement, rendering, accessibility, and world-design complexity because physical exploration is essential to the intended Doom-like experience; combat, guns, and graphic violence remain outside the concept.
