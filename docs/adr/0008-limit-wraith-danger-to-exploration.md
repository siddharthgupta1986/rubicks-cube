# Limit Wraith danger to exploration

Seal focus will pause Void Wraiths and provide unlimited thinking time with unpenalized hints. The campaign will remove the previous move-based pursuit system that punished unproductive turns and hint requests; move counts may remain as optional performance feedback. Real-time danger resumes only when the player returns to exploration, keeping cube learning safe while making movement through the Archive tense.
