# Use real-time Wraith patrol and pursuit

Void Wraiths will move through the Archive, detect Aya, and pursue her during exploration. Contact causes capture: the scene fades and Aya returns to the most recently restored cube seal without losing puzzle progress. Seal focus and designated refuges pause pursuit, while accessibility options provide slower pursuit and a no-chase exploration mode. This supplies the intended corridor-game tension without adding weapons, combat, gore, or punitive loss.
