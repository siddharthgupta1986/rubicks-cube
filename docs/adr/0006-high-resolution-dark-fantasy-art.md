# Use high-resolution stylized dark-fantasy realism

The Archive will use a polished, high-resolution dark-fantasy style with detailed original environments, atmospheric light and fog, smooth environmental motion, and animated Void Wraiths. The game will not use deliberate pixelation or retro low-resolution presentation, and it will favor a cohesive stylized look over photorealism so the required assets and rendering remain achievable.
