# Cube Warden

Cube Warden is a first-person exploration game in which Rubik's Cube puzzles change the traversable world and advance the campaign.

## Language

**Exploration**:
Real-time, player-controlled movement through the Archive from a first-person perspective.
_Avoid_: Route travel, automatic travel, node travel

**Traversal**:
Ground-based movement through the Archive using walking, turning, strafing, and sprinting.
_Avoid_: Platforming, climbing

**Archive**:
The freely walkable game world that connects chambers, passages, and cube seals.
_Avoid_: Illustrated route, route screen

**Sector**:
A distinct region of the Archive containing multiple connected spaces and cube seals.
_Avoid_: Menu chapter, level card

**Unlocked space**:
The portion of the Archive the player can currently explore and revisit. Its boundaries expand as cube seals are restored.
_Avoid_: Selected level

**Cube seal**:
An in-world Rubik's Cube puzzle whose solution changes the Archive and permits further progress.
_Avoid_: Level button, checkpoint node

**Seal sequence**:
The ordered learning progression across twelve cube seals, beginning with individual face turns and ending with a complete solve.
_Avoid_: Level list, difficulty menu

**Seal focus**:
The safe state in which exploration and Void Wraith pursuit pause at a cube seal so the player can manipulate and solve it without leaving the Archive.
_Avoid_: Puzzle page, separate encounter screen

**World change**:
A visible, persistent alteration to the Archive caused by restoring a cube seal, such as opening a passage or activating machinery.
_Avoid_: Continue button, unlocked node

**Checkpoint**:
A safe recovery point created by restoring a cube seal, including the corresponding spawn point and all persistent world changes.
_Avoid_: Exact-position save, autosave slot

**Void Wraith**:
A moving shadow presence that patrols the Archive and pursues Aya after detecting her.
_Avoid_: Monster, combatant

**Capture**:
Contact between Aya and a Void Wraith that returns exploration to the most recently restored cube seal without removing puzzle progress.
_Avoid_: Death, combat defeat

**Pursuit**:
The real-time state in which a Void Wraith has detected Aya and actively follows her through the Archive.
_Avoid_: Cube-move penalty, puzzle timer

**Detection**:
The moment a patrolling Void Wraith sees Aya or reaches close proximity and visibly signals that pursuit has begun.
_Avoid_: Random pursuit, omniscient tracking

**Search**:
The temporary state in which a Void Wraith investigates Aya's last known position after losing sight of her.
_Avoid_: Pursuit

**Refuge**:
An in-world space where Void Wraith pursuit is paused and Aya can safely inspect the environment.
_Avoid_: Combat zone

**Field Kit**:
The collection of cube-learning and standalone play tools outside the campaign.
_Avoid_: Campaign
